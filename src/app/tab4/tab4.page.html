Privacy and Security

The Inventory Management System is committed to protecting your privacy and the security of the data you entrust to us.

Below is an overview of how we meet privacy standards and secure your information.


Privacy Requirements and Compliance

The Inventory Management System complies with the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). We only collect essential personal information and always explain how it is used.

  • Collects minimal information: names, email addresses, and access permissions.
  • Allows you to view, update, or request deletion of your personal data.
  • Provides opt-out options for non-essential communications.

Security Considerations

  • Data Encryption: Encryption for data that is stored and also in transit.
  • Authentication and Authorisation: Strong passwords and optional multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Users only access data and features relevant to their role.
  • Activity Monitoring: System activity is logged and monitored for unusual behaviour.
  • Third-Party Compliance: Any third-party services we use must meet our security standards.
  • Data Deletion: Data is securely deleted when no longer needed.
  • User Awareness: This page exists to keep you informed and in control of your data.

Need Help?

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is managed, please our support team via the contact details listed on the Help page.
